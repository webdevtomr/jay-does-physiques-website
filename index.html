About me

Growing up with lots of issues including asthma, eczema and food allergies, this made me very malnourished and skinny at a young age where it was a concern. Once I got older my weight fluctuated massively making me overweight, I know what it's to be the fat kid at the pool, hating how I looked in any clothes besides a baggy shirt and a hoodie, self conscious of anything I do and feeling insecure. From the age of 11 I started kickboxing to try to keep fit but my diet was lacking as I was eating all kinds of unhealthy food everyday such as fast food and processed sugar. I have lost over 20kg dropping from 95kg to 75kg, I had plenty of times where I wanted to give up as the scale was not going down but slowly realised losing fat isn't always about what the scale says, it's how you feel and look. Since losing weight I have experimented with many ways of losing and gaining weight through diets and exercises, constantly wanting to learn more and eventually I applied to become a personal trainer through AIPT, achieving my Cert 3 and 4 in personal training and fitness, I am now a qualified trainer and currently work out of Goodlife health clubs in Joondalup along with online training. I have been doing kickboxing for just over 8 years now starting in late 2017 and have been regularly competing in local, state and national competitions including winning a national title in 2023, I am also a instructor at Premier Martial arts and fitness. If you ever see me on the gym floor, feel free to come up to me for advice or even just a have a chat or send me a DM on my socials listed under the Contact tab.

Here's how I work

  • We start with an honest chat. Your goals, any injuries, what you've tried before. I need to know where you're at to get you where you want to be.
  • I write your program personally. Every exercise, set, and rep is chosen for you. As you get stronger, the program evolves with you.
  • I track everything. Your lifts, your form, how you're feeling. This data tells us if we're on the right track or need to adjust.
  • Life happens, we adapt. FIFO worker? Busy parent? Injured? I've worked with it all. Your program bends, it doesn't break.
